CDK Global, Inc. (CDK) Ex-Dividend Date Scheduled for June 16, 2017

June 15, 2017, 09:00:04 AM EDT By NASDAQ.com News

Shutterstock photo

CDK Global, Inc. ( CDK ) will begin trading ex-dividend on June 16, 2017. A cash dividend payment of $0.14 per share is scheduled to be paid on June 30, 2017. Shareholders who purchased CDK prior to the ex-dividend date are eligible for the cash dividend payment. This marks the 3rd quarter that CDK has paid the same dividend. At the current stock price of $61.34, the dividend yield is .91%.

The previous trading day's last sale of CDK was $61.34, representing a -9.11% decrease from the 52 week high of $67.49 and a 16.99% increase over the 52 week low of $52.43.

CDK is a part of the Miscellaneous sector, which includes companies such as Alibaba Group Holding Limited ( BABA ) and Visa Inc. ( V ). CDK's current earnings per share, an indicator of a company's profitability, is $1.96. Zacks Investment Research reports CDK's forecasted earnings growth in 2017 as 39.54%, compared to an industry average of 16%.

For more information on the declaration, record and payment dates, visit the CDK Dividend History page. Our Dividend Calendar has the full list of stocks that have an ex-dividend today.

Interested in gaining exposure to CDK through an Exchange Traded Fund [ETF]?
The following ETF(s) have CDK as a top-10 holding:

  • Guggenheim S&P Spin-Off ETF ( CSD )
  • PowerShares Dynamic Software ( PSJ )
  • PowerShares S&P MidCap Low Volatility Portfolio ( XMLV )
  • Vanguard S&P Mid-Cap 400 Growth ETF ( IVOG )
  • iShares S&P Mid-Cap 400 Growth ETF ( IJK ).

The top-performing ETF of this group is PSJ with an increase of 14.43% over the last 100 days. CSD has the highest percent weighting of CDK at 2.96%.
